# Milestone v1.2.0: Lifecycle Hygiene & Ownership

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-24
**Phases:** 35-40
**Total Plans:** 11

## Overview

`v1.2.0` makes lifecycle and ownership first-class operator flows without widening Rulestead beyond its linked-version, two-package shape. This release adds host-friendly ownership metadata, bounded lifecycle classification, archive-readiness guidance built from explicit evidence, a governed mounted-admin archive flow, and the docs plus verification surfaces needed to teach the full “flag from birth to retirement” story with least-surprise defaults.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Activated lifecycle hygiene and ownership as `v1.2.0` ahead of guarded rollout and reusable targeting because the strongest post-GA gap was operator trust and cleanup discipline.
- Treated owner references as opaque host-owned metadata instead of creating a Rulestead-owned identity directory or team graph.
- Kept archive-readiness advisory and evidence-based, never auto-archiving flags or hiding uncertainty behind false precision.
- Preserved the linked-version sibling-package model by keeping runtime/domain contracts in `rulestead` and operator workflows in mounted `rulestead_admin`.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Ownership and lifecycle metadata now survive authored-state writes, reads, audits, and mounted-admin presentation consistently.
- Archive-readiness is now explainable from lifecycle posture, evaluation evidence, and code-reference freshness instead of a blunt stale heuristic.
- Mounted-admin lifecycle review now has a governed preview/confirm archive flow with queue-return outcome handling and audit continuity.
- Lifecycle docs, CLI, and mounted host seams now form one release-facing “birth to retirement” story with targeted verification.
- The missing Phase 35 and Phase 37 verification artifacts were reconstructed and milestone traceability is now complete end to end.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Guarded rollout foundations remain the strongest immediate follow-on milestone candidate after `v1.2.0`.
- Reusable targeting assets remain a later follow-on once rollout and lifecycle posture stay understandable together.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- No new milestone-specific technical debt was accepted at close; the milestone shipped after the missing verification artifacts were reconstructed and the audit marked the evidence chain ready for closeout.

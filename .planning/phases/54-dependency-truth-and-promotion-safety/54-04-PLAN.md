| T-54-20 | Repudiation | Inconsistent blocker evidence | mitigate | Cross-surface parity tests require shared blocker codes and ordering in both adapters. |
| T-54-21 | Elevation of privilege | Package boundary drift | mitigate | Release contract checks assert dependency truth stays in `rulestead` and mounted concerns remain downstream. |
| T-54-22 | Information Disclosure | Support checklist overexposure | mitigate | Handoff checklist mandates redacted evidence and no raw traits in logs/audit/telemetry. |
| T-54-23 | Denial of Service | Verification gaps | mitigate | `mix verify.phase54` aggregates all merge-blocking proof suites into one reliable gate. |
| T-54-24 | Tampering | Runtime purity drift | mitigate | Include runtime snapshot contract checks to ensure no new dependency lookup path was introduced. |

<objective>
Encode the D-12/D-13/D-14 publish mechanics as a scripts-first pre-publish guard (`scripts/ci/openfeature_publish_guard.sh`) plus an ordered runbook in MAINTAINING.md's existing manual-step section. The guard and runbook together make the silent-path-drop footgun (D-14) impossible to hit by accident: the guard refuses to proceed unless the env gate is set and `rulestead` resolves as a real Hex dep, and the runbook mandates the `mix hex.publish --dry-run` dependency-list inspection as the final human catch.

This plan builds the guardrail before plan 129-03 pulls the irreversible trigger.

Output: An executable guard script and an updated MAINTAINING.md runbook — both autonomous, committable to `main`, and consumed by plan 129-03.
</objective>
